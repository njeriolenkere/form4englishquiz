Form 4 English quiz

Choose the correct word to complete the following sentences.

  1. Satan, along with his evil followers _____ always searching for new ways to mislead mankind.

    a) Is b) Are

  2. Tea, served with bacon, eggs and sausages, _____ one in a fine mood in the morning.

    a) Puts b) Put

  3. She knew all about my friend and _____ .

    a) I b) Me

  4. What would you do if you were _____?

    a) She b) Her

  5. If there is tea left in that flask, I would like to have _____

    a) Little b) A little

  6. Since animal proteins are very expensive, _____ people eat enough.

    a) A few b) Few

  7. 7. She is a new employee; _____ , she has done very well.

    a) Nevertheless b) Although

  8. 8. Jason seems to be quite intelligent; _____, he often gets poor grades.

    a) Similarly b) However

  9. If they had not _____ him money, he would not have gone for the trip.

    a) Send b) Sent

  10. Let's _____ our clothes here.

    a) Hang b) Hung
